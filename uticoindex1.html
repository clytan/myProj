INDUSTRIAL WATER TREATMENT

Water treatment is used to optimize most water-based industrial processes, such as: heating, cooling, processing, cleaning, and rinsing, so that operating costs and risks are reduced. Effluent water from one process might be perfectly suitable for reuse in another process somewhere else. With the proper treatment, a significant proportion of industrial on-site wastewater might be reusable.
Utico has extensive knowledge of cooling water treatment for open and closed systems in industrial and commercial facilities, including refineries, chemical plants, ammonia plants, tire plants, paper mills, corn milling operations, sugar refineries, pharmaceutical plants, power plants automotive plants, breweries, warehouses and office buildings.
